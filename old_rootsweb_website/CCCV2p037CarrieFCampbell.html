Campbell Cousins Correspondence

525 W. Gray Street,

Elmira, N. Y.

April 8, 1924

Dear Cousins:

As I review the past months since I last wrote you there seems nothing of much interest to tell you about. Undoubtedly many of you know I have disposed of our former home here and am now occupying a four-room, heated apartment. Certainly this is a great change, but I rather enjoy it. One pleasant feature is that I am not more than fifteen minutes walk from my son William's home, and only a block out of his way to business. As I have continued my work at the office, it makes it very nice for me as he comes for me in the morning and we return together at night.

Harry and family have spent the winter in Paris, and there is a possibility they will remain in France another year. J. Harry Campbell was married to Catharine DeWitt Chambers of Port Jefferson, N. Y, September 17, 1913, They have two children, Catherine Sebring Campbell born May 10, 1915 and Jane Mather Campbell born April 19, 1917.

William S. Campbell was married to Winifred Tobey of Elmira, N. Y., April 15, 1914. They have one son, Wilfred Sebring Campbell, born August 26, 1916.

Well, Cousins, it is a pleasure to hear from you all as it recalls many good times some of us have been favored to enjoy together.

With very best wishes to you all.

Sincerely,

COUSIN CARRIE F. CAMPBELL.
